My Journey Around the World

Hello and welcome to my travel journey! I’m from China, a country rich in history, culture, and cuisine, but I’ve always felt the urge to explore the world beyond my borders. Traveling has always been a dream of mine, not just to see famous landmarks, but to experience the true essence of different cultures—whether it’s through the food, the people, or the unique way each place tells its own story.
